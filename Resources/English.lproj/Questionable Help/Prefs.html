Preferences

Questionable has three preference tabs: Game, Sounds and Registration.

Game

"Length of game" is fairly self-explainatory. This is how much time is allowed for the players to go through all the questions. Once this much time has passed (not counting time paused), the game will end immediately.

"Time to answer question" is multi-purpose. It is the amount of time that players are given to buzz in when a question is shown. It is also the amount of time a player has to answer a question once they buzz in. If this time is too short for a particular question (i.e. if the question takes a long time to read) or you think a player needs more time to answer, pause the game and continue once you feel you have given enough time.



Sounds

Select the name of the sound you want to play for each event.

The sounds listed are all the .aiff and .wav files in the system sound directories. If you want to add your own sounds create an .aiff or .wav sound file (there are lots of free apps for converting sound files) and put it into one of these directories:

  • /System/Library/Sounds
  • ~/Library/Sounds (a.k.a. - The library in your home directory)

In general you should put the sounds in the second directory listed unless you have a good reason not to.



Registration

This displays your registration info. If you have not registered, this will just display "Unregistered."
