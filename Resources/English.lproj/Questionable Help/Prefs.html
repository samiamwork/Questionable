Preferences

Questionable has two preference tabs: Game and Registration.

Game

"Length of game" is fairly self-explainatory. This is how much time is allowed for the players to go through all the questions. Once this much time has passed (not counting time paused), the game will end immediately.

"Time to answer question" is somewhat multi-purpose. It is the amount of time that players are given to buzz in when a question is shown. It is also the amount of time a player has to answer a question once they buzz in. If this time is too short for a particular question (i.e. if the question takes a long time to read) or you think a player needs more time to answer, pause the game and continue once you feel you have given enough time.



Registration

This displays your registration info. If you have not registered, this will just display "Unregistered."
